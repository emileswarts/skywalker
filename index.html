Skywalker vim colourscheme

This is a dark colourscheme that I made over the course of a month. I compiled the palette from a colours that just work together. It consists of a wide range of greys, purples, red for search highlights, aqua blue cursor, army menu completion text, and a complimentary orange here and there. Inspired(heavily) by (@sjl) Badwolf colourscheme.

git clone git@github.com:emileswarts/skywalker.git ~/skywalker
mv ~/skywalker/skywalker.vim ~/.vim/colors
vim ~/.vimrc
change to colorscheme skywalker
